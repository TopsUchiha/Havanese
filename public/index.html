About Us We LOVE our dogs! We have been showing, breeding, and sharing our lives with Havanese for almost 15 years. Our dogs live in our home with us as our treasured family pets. We don't have puppies all the time, but when we do, they are raised in our living room where they get daily love and attention from our family.
